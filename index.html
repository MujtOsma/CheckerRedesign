FAQ

Common
Questions.

How do I book a ride with Checker?
You can book in three ways: call our 24/7 booking agents at (519) 659-0400, download the free GoChecker app on iOS or Android, or email us at info@gochecker.ca. The app is the fastest — you'll have a car on the way in under 10 seconds.
What is "Know Before You Go" pricing?
Checker is the only service in London that gives you your fare upfront — before you confirm the booking. No meters. No surprises. You know exactly what you'll pay before your driver arrives, every time.
Is there surge pricing at busy times?
Never. Checker does not use surge pricing — ever. Whether it's New Year's Eve, a snowstorm, or a Friday night, your fare is your fare. Flat rates, always.
Can I schedule a ride in advance?
Absolutely. You can schedule a ride days or weeks in advance through the GoChecker app or by calling (519) 659-0400. We strongly recommend advance booking for airport transfers to guarantee your pickup time.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards through the GoChecker app (no debit fees), GoChecker Gift Cards, corporate account billing, and cash directly with your driver.
Do you serve London International Airport (YXU)?
Yes — airport transfers are one of our most popular services. We provide door-to-door service to and from London International Airport (YXU) with luggage assistance and advance scheduling. Call (519) 659-0400 to plan your airport trip.
How do I set up a business account?
Fill out the business inquiry form above, or call us at (519) 659-0400. Business accounts get priority dispatch, automated digital receipts, and dedicated agent support.
What vehicles are in the Checker fleet?
Our fleet includes the 2023 GMC Yukon Denali XL (7-seat, black), 2022 Lexus RX 350 (white and black), and 2020+ Toyota Highlander XLE (7-seat, white and black). All vehicles feature all-black leather interiors maintained in showroom condition.
Where is Checker located?
Our offices are at 573 Admiral Court, London, ON, Canada. We serve all of London and surrounding areas. Reach us at (519) 659-0400 or info@gochecker.ca — available 24/7.
